Data Deletion Request

In accordance with Facebook's data deletion requirements, you can request the deletion of all data ChatGenius has received about you through Facebook Login.

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

- Your account information (name, email, profile picture)
- Chat conversation history
- Booking and lead data associated with your account

To request data deletion, please email privacy@chatgenius.ai with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation once your data has been deleted.

You can also manage your Facebook app connections at Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites.
