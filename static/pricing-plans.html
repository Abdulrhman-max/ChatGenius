Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans

Can I change my plan later?+

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and any price differences are prorated automatically.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to Pro features. No credit card required to start. Experience everything ChatGeniusX has to offer before committing.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. All payments are processed securely through our payment partner.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer refunds?+

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days of your paid subscription, contact us for a full refund. See our refund policy for more details.

What happens when I exceed my conversation limit?+

You'll receive a notification when you're approaching your conversation limit. Once reached, you can upgrade to a higher plan to continue without interruption. We'll never cut off your service without warning.
