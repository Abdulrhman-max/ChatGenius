Safety

How We Prevent Hallucinations

In healthcare, accuracy is non-negotiable. Our multi-layered safety system ensures the AI only responds with verified, domain-relevant information.

1

RAG with 61+ Dental Knowledge Entries

Every response is grounded in a curated knowledge base of dental procedures, insurance info, and practice-specific data. The AI retrieves relevant context before generating any response.

2

Intent Classification with Confidence Scoring

Each message is classified into 19 healthcare-specific intents with a confidence score. Low-confidence messages are flagged for special handling rather than guessing.

3

Domain-Restricted Responses

The AI is strictly confined to healthcare and practice-related topics. Off-topic requests are politely redirected. No medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations are ever made.

4

Human Handoff When Confidence < 0.6

When the AI's confidence drops below 60%, it automatically escalates to a human agent. No guessing, no hallucinating -- just a smooth handoff to your team.

5

Emergency Detection & Fast-Track

The system detects emergency situations (severe pain, trauma, allergic reactions) and immediately provides emergency contact info and escalation protocols.

6

Response Verification Pipeline

Generated responses pass through a restriction filter and content validator before reaching the patient, ensuring no harmful or inappropriate content is delivered.
